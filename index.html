Mission

Our mission is to eliminate hardware cost as a barrier to high‑precision radiotherapy. We steward an open global standard so that hospitals worldwide — from community sites in the US/EU to LMIC centers — can activate stereotactic capability on the existing installed base of LINACs.

~16,000RT machines worldwide
>60%lack volumetric IGRT
0$software cost for LMICs

What we build

  • OpenBeam X: the nonprofit foundation that governs the open stereotactic guidance standard.
  • PrecisionGrid Engine: the AI reference implementation that turns EPID + log files into synthetic 3D geometric certainty.
  • Certification & QA: enterprise‑grade audits, safety documentation, and training for health systems that need liability assurance.

Why EPID‑AI?

  • Continuous in‑beam feedback (not a single pre‑beam snapshot)
  • Zero additional imaging dose
  • Couch‑agnostic (works with standard 3‑DOF couches)
  • Stronger online adaptation potential than CBCT

Equity model

The core software remains open and free for LMICs. High‑income health systems pay only for certification, QA audits, and integration support. This tiered model guarantees universal access while sustaining the work.
